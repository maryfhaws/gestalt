Girl with Balloon is one of street artist Banksy's most famous works, and its subject is recognizable because of the Gestalt principle of closure. The girl in the piece is composed of different monochromatic shapes that are perceived together as a figure.

The product page for the Apple iPhone depicts the newest phone, but only partially when the page loads. Because the image is partially hidden, users are invited to scroll down to view the rest of the image. This placement illustrates the principle of closure because users maintain a sense of the whole even when part of an image is obscured.
